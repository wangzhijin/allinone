How to bet?

•You can select the match number you want to bet, enter it in the [No.]text frame, then select your prediction (win, fail, draw), then enter the amount you want to bet into [wager]textframe, and finally press the [buy] button. Your one bet is complete.

•After you complete a bet, sometimes the result is slower in response to the list of matches. You can click Refresh to view the [My Holdings] column in the list and see the amount you bet and the bonus pool for this match.

•In the group round robin, players bet on the host team's winning or losing. After the results of each match are released, the system takes 10% of the winners as a handling fee, and the remaining amount will be allocated to the winners.

•During the elimination match, the player bets the winning or losing of the host team. After the results of each match are released, the system takes 10% of the winners as a handling fee, and the remaining amount will be allocated to the winners.

•Each match bet will end before the start of the match and the prize will be awarded after the match is over.

•If you are distributed a bonus, the system will not automatically distribute bonuses to your account. In the [My Account Info] area, your No convertible bonus will be displayed. Please click the [Withdraw My bonous] button to withdraw your bonus.
